Privacy policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who we are

NeuroPhysics is an educational app designed to help GCSE students — particularly neurodivergent learners — revise Physics. The app is operated by the developer ("we", "us", "our").

2. What data we collect

We collect as little data as possible:

  • Account information — your email address is collected when you create an account or sign in with Google or Apple, and is stored securely by Supabase (our authentication provider).
  • Learning progress (topics started, mastered, XP, streak) — stored locally on your device only.
  • Accessibility preferences (reduce motion, high contrast, font settings) — stored on your device and, for signed-in users, may sync to your account so the same settings follow you across devices.
  • Chat messages sent to the Mamo AI tutor — transmitted to Google's Gemini API to generate a response. Recent chat threads may also stay on your device locally so you can continue a conversation later. We do not store your chat history on our own servers.

We do not collect your name, precise location, or payment information.

3. AI-generated content

The Mamo chat feature uses Gemini, an AI model made by Google (google.com). Messages you send are processed by Google's servers to generate responses. Google's Privacy policy applies to this processing: policies.google.com/privacy

All Mamo responses are generated by AI and should not be treated as a substitute for teacher advice.

4. How we store data

Learning progress is stored locally on your device. If you sign in, comfort settings may also sync to your account so they stay consistent across devices. Recent Mamo chat threads may remain in local device storage until you clear them. Clearing the app's data or deleting the app removes local stored data permanently.

5. Children's privacy

NeuroPhysics is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app without parental consent.

Parents or guardians who believe their child under 13 has used the app may contact us to request data deletion.

6. Your rights

You can request account deletion in Settings. You can also delete locally stored data by clearing app data or deleting the app from your device.

For any questions about your data, contact us at the support address below.

7. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API — powers the Mamo AI tutor. Messages are sent to Google's servers. See policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Supabase — provides authentication (account login). Your email address is stored on Supabase's servers. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • Vercel — hosts the API proxy. Vercel may log request metadata (IP address, timestamps). See vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at neurophysics.co.uk/privacy and within the app under Settings → Privacy policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please contact:
support@neurophysics.app
